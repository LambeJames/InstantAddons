    A misplaced or stolen car key is often only the start of your problems. Most people keep keys in sets, meaning when they lose one they lose them all.

    • You realise your keys have been lost or stolen. Don't panic!

    • You call the Keycare emergency hotline

    • We arrange for an authorised locksmith to come let you in

    How does Keycare work?

    With Keycare, we'll cover all your keys - simply call our emergency helpline and we'll let you in to your house, car or business premises.

    • Cover you for keys that have been permanently lost, stolen, or locked in the building or vehicle
    • Cover for replacement locks or any locksmith charges
    • Reprogramming of your immobiliser
    • Onward travel costs, to get you to your original destination
    • Up to 3 days vehicle hire, if your vehicle is unusable as a result of lost or stolen keys
    • 24-hour assistance, 365 days a year
    • No expensive or hidden excesses

    I thought you included replacement locks as standard?

    We do, but replacement locks only cover your car keys up to the value of £500. If your other keys are lost or stolen you'll have to call-out a locksmith and that can be expensive. If you've lost multiple keys, then the expense can really ramp up.

    Having Keycare means you've got peace of mind knowing that all your keys are covered.

    What else is there to know?

    • Claims must be reported within 30 days
    • Claims relating to damaged keys or locks, or the result of wear and tear are not covered
    • Lost keys will usually require a period of three days before being replaced
    • On occasion we might not be able to arrange a locksmith ourselves, if that happens you'll be required to arrange one and we'll reimburse the cost (up to £1000 once receipts are provided)
